1. Main Features & Services Offered

The Application offers a wide range of mobile financial and transactional services conducted seamlessly between users of the TAP System, including the ability to perform:

Airtime Recharge Perform instant Flexiload top-ups for yourself or any other mobile number using your wallet balance. Save favorites for quick reuse.
Utility Bill Pay Pay utility bills through designated TAP Bill Pay Service Partners and verify your instant payment status dashboard.
Send Money & Cash Out Transfer balances instantly to any active TAP wallet or withdraw hard cash through authorized TAP agent points.
Add Money Load funds directly into your digital wallet from verified associated bank accounts or credit cards.
Merchant & Tuition Payments Scan dynamic QR codes to settle retail purchases, e-commerce checkouts, and institutional tuition fees securely.
Account Dashboard Features Check real-time balances, view transaction limits (count/amount), and track up to your last 50 historical statements.

2. Registration and Account Setup

To access the full spectrum of TAP Services, users are required to register their active mobile number, verify identity data, and initialize a unique secure wallet PIN.

  • Authentication relies on an automatic One-Time Password (OTP) validation framework captured natively by the system environment.
  • Certain legacy devices or operating systems may require users to manually input the received OTP to complete the handshake.

3. User Security Duties

Security is a shared responsibility. You must take all reasonable precautions to keep your physical mobile device safe and prevent fraudulent use of your active account profile:

  • Never write down, extract, or share your PIN/security details in a format readable by third parties.
  • Avoid creating simple or easily guessable security credentials.
  • Log out of the TAP application immediately when your tasks are finished; do not leave the active app loop processing in the background unattended.
  • The user remains solely responsible for all programmatic operations authorized from their device between login and explicit logout.
Critical System Warning: Due to the virtual nature of digital banking structures and external exposure to threat actors, inherent system risks exist. Do not disclose credentials to anyone claiming to represent Law Enforcement Agencies or TAP administration.

4. Personal Data & Confidentiality

For identity validation, compliance, and core utility processing, TAP collects and maintains strict confidentiality over essential personal data variables, including but not limited to:

  • Full legal name, gender, date of birth, and email profile address.
  • National Identity (NID) database records, copy documents, or valid Driving License numbers.
  • Account balance variations and user transaction histories.

Adequate administrative safeguards are constantly deployed to insulate your records against unauthorized access vectors.
