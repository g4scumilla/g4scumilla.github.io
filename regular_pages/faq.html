Frequently Asked Questions

Use the Resignation Automations link on the dashboard to submit your resignation easily.
2. How can I prepare an advance resignation letter?
Click on Prepare Advance Resignation Letter in the dashboard links.
3. Where can I find the training sheets?
Use the Prepare Training Sheet link on the dashboard to access training sheets.
